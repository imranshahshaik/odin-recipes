Talbina Recipe

Introduction

Talbina gives comfort to the aggrieved heart and it lessens grief. Talbina is a porridge made from barley flour, formed by adding milk and honey to the dried barley powder. Historically, the flatbread used was probably stale and unleavened.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Barley
  • 1 litre Milk
  • 1/2 cup Dates/ honey
  • 2 tbsp Chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Wash barley well and soak for 3-4 hours.
  2. In a pan, bring milk to a rolling boil and add the soaked barley.
  3. Let it boil for 10 minutes.
  4. Now add chopped and deseeded dates for sweetness. If you are using honey, add honey in the last step.
  5. Cook for 10 more minutes.
  6. Add chopped nuts and dry fruits of your choice once almost done.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
